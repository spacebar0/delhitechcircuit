About Us

Delhi Tech Circuit (D.T.C) is a coalition of the most prominent and innovative high school tech clubs in Delhi. United by a shared passion for technology, D.T.C brings together the brightest young minds to collaborate and create extraordinary opportunities for students across the city. We specialize in hosting high-impact events, such as Dn3xt, which foster creativity, technical skill, and innovation in a competitive environment. Our mission is to empower the next generation of tech enthusiasts by providing platforms for learning, collaboration, and showcasing talent.

ABOUT D-N3xt

Hosted by D.T.C, the Dn3xt event brings together the brightest tech minds from high schools across the city. This competition is designed to challenge and inspire the next generation of innovators, providing a unique platform to test your skills.
